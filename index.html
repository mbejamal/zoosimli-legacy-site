About Us

Zoosimli Legacy Tech and Roots Center is a community-driven initiative dedicated to preserving the rich cultural heritage of Northern Ghana while equipping youth with modern digital skills.

We combine tradition and technology to empower communities, promote cultural identity, and create sustainable opportunities for growth.

Our Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To empower youth and communities through cultural education, digital skills training, and sustainable development initiatives rooted in Northern Ghanaian heritage.

Our Vision

To become a leading center for cultural preservation and technological empowerment, connecting tradition with innovation for global impact.
